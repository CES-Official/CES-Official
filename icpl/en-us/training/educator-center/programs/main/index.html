Teach AI with Microsoft Applied Skills

Microsoft Applied Skills is the easiest way to bring technical skilling into your classroom while allowing your students to work on real-world tech skills. Getting started is as easy as:

  • Select the Microsoft Applied Skills you want to use in your course and assign the Applied Skill to your students.
  • Your students can work through the learning path and confirm their understanding by passing the assessment.
  • By passing the online assessment students will receive a Microsoft verified credential that will help them stand out as they prepare for their career.

Why use Microsoft Applied Skills in your Course?

Online, Free, and Easy to Integrate

You have the freedom to choose which Microsoft Applied Skills compliment or expand your in-class teaching and easily assign them to your students.

Enable students to efficiently demonstrate learned skills using practical scenarios and interactive lab-based assessments.

A path to Microsoft Credentials

Ensure your students stand out in their professional journey by helping them earn a credential validating their skills which is verified by Microsoft.
