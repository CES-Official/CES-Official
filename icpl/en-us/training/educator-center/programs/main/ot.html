OfficeTRAINER Practice Tests for MOS

These real-time, live-in-the-application, performance-based assessment, and test preparation tools help individuals achieve MOS certification credentials. The OfficeTRAINER reinforces learning and validates knowledge for students, instructors, career colleges, and technical training facilities.

OfficeTRAINER practice exams are mapped to MOS certification exam objectives for the 2013 and 2016 versions. Everything you need to know to pass your certification exam is provided in this feature-rich test engine.

Many students are anxious to assess their knowledge of applications prior to taking the certification exams. Additionally, we recognize the need for educators to properly assess an individual’s knowledge. Cyberlearning provides OfficeTRAINER Practice Tests. These tests ensure your students are adequately prepared to take their certification exam.

Why OfficeTRAINER Practice Tests?

  • Assess knowledge gaps and identify strengths
  • Feature-rich test engine that is mapped to ODs
  • Boost student confidence and increase certification exam pass rates
  • Advanced reporting helps students study targeted topics)
  • Prepares students for exam-taking conditions
  • Enhance learning through self-paced practice questions

OfficeTRAINER is available in two modes:

  • Training Mode, and
  • Testing Mode

Training Mode

  • Students work at their own pace as they receive feedback and step-by-step instructions for each question from the help feature
  • Questions cover the exam objectives in a similar format and style to the exam
  • Allows sufficient time to cover the exam while referring to help option
  • Allows global book marking

Testing mode:

  • Students experience what the certification exams will be like
  • Timed practice tests present scenarios to perform just like the actual certification exam
