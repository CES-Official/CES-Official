Microsoft Learn for Educators

Equip your students with AI and tech skills for today—and tomorrow​

With Microsoft Learn for Educators (MSLE), faculty members can easily integrate AI Skills, technical skills, and industry-trusted and verified credentials into their curriculum to set students up for real-world success.

To access Microsoft Official Curriculum and teaching materials, select the sign-up button below.

Three flexible ways to bring Microsoft AI and tech skills into your classroom

  • Teach AI with Microsoft Applied Skills

    Easily enhance your course with Microsoft Applied Skills. Select the AI and tech skills you would like your students to learn and assign the Microsoft Applied Skill. The interactive learning experiences prepare them to earn a Microsoft verified credential that validates in-demand skills.

    Learn more
  • Utilize Microsoft teaching resources and educator support

    When you join Microsoft Learn for educators you gain access to resources like ready-to-teach curriculum, lab environments, and Microsoft Official Courseware. Not only do you get the resources you want, but you have access to support when you need it.

    Learn more
  • Join AI Bootcamp for Educators to get the AI skills you need to teach your classes

    Delivered through our global network of Training Service Partners (TSPs), Microsoft AI Bootcamps for Educators can help you learn the AI skills you need to teach your own students practical AI skills with Microsoft Azure AI technology. Further, our TSPs can help you integrate AI skills into your program or curriculum and provide bespoke onboarding to MSLE.

    Get connected

Microsoft AI Bootcamps for Educators prepare you to bring AI Skills to your classroom

With Microsoft AI Bootcamps for Educators, you will work with a Microsoft Training Service Partner who will help you learn the AI skills you need to teach your own students practical AI skills with Microsoft Azure AI technology. Through this bespoke, fee-based consultative engagement you will:

  • Build capability to integrate AI skills development into your existing curricula.
  • Gain skills in Azure AI tools and technologies
  • Be provided guided curriculum integration
  • Receive guidance on earning industry-recognized Microsoft AI credentials
Learn more

Benefits of working with a Training Service Partner

Curriculum Integration

Microsoft Training Services Partners can evaluate your current curriculum and education requirements and work with you on how to best integrate the appropriate AI technical skilling to enhance your curriculum.

Faster for Implementation

When you partner with a Training Services Partner, you are working with people that know the technology and can help you get prepared to teach your students. This lets you reduce prep time and increase instruction time.

Skilling for all Microsoft technology

With experience working with all Microsoft technologies, Training Services Partners are able to help you not only bring AI skilling to your students, but can assist with classroom instruction for Dynamics 365, Azure, Azure Data, security / cybersecurity, Power Platform, and more.

If you need help bringing AI skills into your classroom, get connected with Training Services Partners to learn about AI Bootcamp for Educators.
