About Us
A Trusted Microsoft Global Training Partner

At CyberLearning, we envision a future where education drives transformation, unlocking potential and creating pathways for success in a globalized world. Established in 2007, we have become a premier provider of cutting-edge IT learning solutions, delivering impactful training and certifications across India and beyond.

As a Global Training Partner of Microsoft, CyberLearning delivers innovative programs such as:

  • AI for Educators Bootcamps
  • Cybersecurity & AI Bootcamps
  • Prompt Engineering and Prompt-a-thon Bootcamps
  • Co-Pilot Training

We have pioneered the introduction of globally recognized certifications in India, including the Microsoft Office Specialist (MOS), Microsoft Technology Associate (MTA), and Adobe Certified Associate (ACA), among others. Through a network of over 1,000 authorized testing centers, we created opportunities for students, educators, and professionals to gain skills that bridge the gap between education and real-world applications. Till date, we have delivered over 3,00,000 certification exams.

CyberLearning’s achievements include executing large-scale projects in partnership with Microsoft, such as delivering IT training to polytechnics and ITIs across 18 Indian states, empowering over 20,000 teachers and students with digital literacy, and hosting transformative programs like Project Oorja and the Rotary-Microsoft 21st Century Skills Initiative.

We are proud to host COMPUDON, India’s gateway to the Microsoft Office Specialist World Championship. This initiative has provided Indian students with a platform to showcase their expertise on a global stage, earning accolades such as the first-ever gold and silver medals for India at the Microsoft Office World Championship.

Our mission extends beyond imparting skills. We aim to promote a culture of lifelong learning, enabling individuals to reach their full potential and make meaningful contributions to their communities. CyberLearning also developed technology-driven solutions like Prometheus, our cloud-based exam engine, offering a streamlined experience for institutions worldwide.

Our experiential learning product, OfficeTRAINER mitigates the forgetting curve, results in concrete learning & enhances retention by bridging the gap between learning and doing.

CyberLearning is more than a training company; we see ourselves as a catalyst for transformation.
